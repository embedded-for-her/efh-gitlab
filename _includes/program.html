Program

What?

10-week intensive course

{{site.title}} is an intensive 80-hour program in embedded systems with emphasis on learning practical hands-on skills.

How?

Theory, practicals & projects

The 1st half of the program contains lectures intertwined with hands-on tasks. As we believe in learning by doing, the 2nd half consists of a team-based project work.

Who?

Women and minorities in tech

Women and other minorities in tech who have a basic background in either computer science or electronics.

When?

Saturdays

Every Saturday from 10th June to 12th Aug. The whole day from 10:00 to 18:00.

Cost?

Rs. 5000/-

The program fee includes the hardware provided to the participants, which includes a development board, soldering-gun, multimeter and much more.

Where?

Women in engineering

Sessions will be held all over Bengaluru in various start-ups, makerspaces and community centres.

Only 12 participants!
