Schedule

Embedded for Her will be held on Saturdays for 10 weeks from June to Aug 2016.

  • {Start Date}

    Application period

    Registrations for this program will open up in June 2016. Sign up using this form and we'll let you know when it opens.

  • Week 1-4, 6-8

    Course work

    The course work will start off with a hands-on session with Arduino to build a simple application. After that would be the learning bare metal embedded coding, peripheral interfacing including GPIOs, timers and serial communication protocols. Hands-on tasks will be included for every topic.

  • Week 5

    Course mid evaluation
    & Project initiation

    An evaluation will be conducted to assess the progress of this pilot program, get feedback and make necessary correction. Participants will get feedback on their progress.

  • Week 6-9

    Project work

    The last weekend will be a two day event where we'll give the final touches to the project for the show & tell event. This event will be attended by start-ups in the embedded space whom you can impress.

  • Week 10

    Final sprint

    The last weekend will be a two day event where we'll give the final touches to the project for the show & tell event. This event will be attended by start-ups in the embedded space whom you can impress.
