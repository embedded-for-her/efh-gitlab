Schedule

Embedded for Her will be held on Saturdays for 10 weeks from 10th June to 12th Aug 2017.

  • Till 15th May

    Application period

    Registrations for this program will be open untill 15th May 2017. Sign up at the registration section.

  • Week 1-5

    Coursework

    The coursework will start off with a hands-on session learning the basic components that make up a typical circuit. After that would be learning bare metal embedded coding, peripheral interfacing including GPIOs, timers and serial communication protocols. Hands-on tasks will be included for every topic.

  • Week 5

    Project initiation

    Teams of 3 need to be formed and a project needs to be decided by each team. This is the project that the team would work on, right from deciding the end goal, choosing the technology to use and the actual implementation and testing.

  • Week 6-9

    Project work

    Work with mentors to decide your project's roadmap, understand what to learn and debug the problem that's keeping you awake.

  • Week 10

    Final sprint

    The last weekend will be a two-day event where we'll give the final touches to the project for the show & tell event. Impress all the attendees that come to this event.
